Privacy Policy

Best By Manager is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your device.

What We Access

  • Camera – Used to take photos of products. Images are stored locally only.
  • Internet – Used to retrieve public product data from Open Food Facts.
  • Storage – Required for exporting inventory reports (optional).
  • Notifications – Required to show expiration reminders.

No Cloud Sync

All data remains on your device. There are no analytics, ads, or tracking scripts.

Third-Party Services

Product data and images are retrieved from Open Food Facts, licensed under the Open Database License (ODbL) and Creative Commons BY-SA 3.0.
