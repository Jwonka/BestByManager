Privacy Policy

Best By Manager is committed to protecting your privacy.
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your device.

What We Access

  • Camera – Used to scan barcodes and take product photos. Images are stored locally only.
  • Internet – Used only to retrieve public product data from Open Food Facts.
  • Notifications – Used for expiration reminders and optional 7-day early warning alerts (Android 13+).

No ads. No analytics. No tracking.

No Cloud Sync

All inventory data remains on your device. You control your data.

Account Recovery (Local-Only)

Forgot your password?

  • Self-service reset: If device screen lock/biometrics are enabled and recovery is turned on by an admin, you can reset your password on the login screen.
  • Admin reset: Administrators can reset user passwords in-app.
  • Last resort (data wipe): Administrators can wipe local app data to return the app to first-run state.

Wiping data permanently deletes all inventory and user data and cannot be undone.

Third-Party Services
