Best By Manager

Keep track of your fresh produce and products with Best By Manager.

What Can Best By Manager Do?

  • Scan barcodes to quickly identify food items
  • Add products with expiration dates, categories, and quantities
  • Track expiration by employee or category
  • Get smart reports for products expiring soon
  • Use the camera to snap product photos for easy ID

How It Works

  • Install the APK and launch the app
  • Create the first user – becomes the administrator automatically
  • Add inventory via “Add Product” or by scanning a barcode
  • Search and filter reports by barcode, date, or employee
  • Export or share expiration summaries as plain text
