Best By Manager

Keep food safe and shelves stocked with Best By Manager
🧊 Kiosk-style expiration tracking for small businesses.

What Can Best By Manager Do?

  • Kiosk workflow — employees select themselves and enter a PIN; all actions are tracked under their account
  • Scan barcodes to identify items fast (Open Food Facts lookup)
  • Add products with brand, weight, category, quantity, and expiration date
  • Early warning reminders — tap the bell icon on the expiration field to enable a 7-day pre-expiry alert
  • Expiration alarms fire on the expiration date and clear automatically when quantity hits 0
  • Discard tracking (quantity + optional reason) with reporting totals
  • Reports filtered by barcode, date range, expiration status, or employee
  • Camera photos stored locally — replace a product photo at any time from the overflow menu
  • Local-only storage — no cloud accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics
  • Admin tools — password reset, PIN reset, employee management, ownership transfer, and a last-resort data wipe
  • Offline mode — disable barcode lookup for environments without reliable internet
  • Dark / light theme — owner-controlled toggle

How It Works

  • Install the app and create the first account → becomes the Owner automatically
  • Add employees from the Administrator Dashboard → Employee Details
  • Employees select their name on the kiosk screen and set a PIN on first use
  • Add inventory via Product Details or by scanning a barcode
  • Enable early warning by tapping the bell icon on the expiration field
  • Record discards for expired or damaged items from the overflow menu
  • Run reports and share summaries via the Android share sheet
  • Device auto-locks after inactivity and returns to the employee selection screen
