About Best By Manager

Best By Manager is an Android app for grocery stores, gas stations, and small businesses to track product inventory and expiration dates. It supports barcode lookup, product photos, reporting, discard tracking, and reminders — while keeping all inventory data stored locally on the device.

Why It Matters

Expired food is costly and unsafe. Best By Manager helps you keep stock accurate, reduce waste, and catch items before they expire — including optional 7-day early warning reminders.

Who It’s For

  • Grocery stores & markets
  • Convenience stores & gas stations
  • Food pantries & stockrooms
