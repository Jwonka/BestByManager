About Best By Manager

Best By Manager is an Android app designed for grocery stores, gas stations, and small businesses to keep track of product inventory and expiration dates. The app helps reduce waste, ensure food safety, and maintain accurate stock levels through smart features like barcode lookup, image capture, reporting, and offline support.

Why It Matters

Wasting expired food is costly and unsafe.
Best By Manager makes it easy to manage products before they go bad—helping your business stay compliant and efficient.

Who It’s For

  • Grocery stores & markets
  • Convenience stores & gas stations
  • Food pantries & stockrooms
